Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 April 2026

This policy explains what information Craftsky collects, why we collect it, and what we do with it. We've tried to write it in plain language. If anything is unclear, email support@craftsky.com.

Who we are

Craftsky is a social platform for textile crafters built on the AT Protocol — a federated, open network. When you read "we" or "us" in this policy, that's the team building Craftsky.

What we collect

Information you give us directly

  • Waiting-list email address. If you sign up to be told when Craftsky launches, we collect the email address you give us. We use it only to send you that update.
  • Account information. When Craftsky launches, you'll sign in with an AT Protocol identity (e.g. a Bluesky handle). We store the minimum needed to keep you signed in — a Craftsky session token, plus the OAuth tokens needed to write to your repository on your Personal Data Server (PDS). We do not store your PDS password; we never see it.
  • Anything you write to us. Support emails, feedback, bug reports — all stored in the inbox they were sent to.

Information your device shares automatically

  • Anonymous usage data. We use PostHog to understand how the website and app are used — which pages people visit, which buttons they press, how the product flows. PostHog is configured for anonymous, no-cookie tracking. We do not link this to any user identity.
  • Server logs. Like any website, our servers log basic information about requests (IP address, user agent, time of request) for security and debugging. These logs are kept for a short period.

Information from the AT Protocol

Posts, follows, likes, profile information — anything you write through Craftsky to your AT Protocol repository — are public by design. They live on your PDS, are broadcast through the firehose, and can be read by any AT Protocol app, including ours. We index this public data so we can show it to you and to other Craftsky users. We do not control this data; it lives on the network.

If you want to stop sharing this data, you can delete records from your PDS, or change which PDS hosts your account. The Craftsky app does not host your records.

What we don't collect

  • No advertising data. We don't run ads. We don't share data with ad networks.
  • No tracking pixels in email. Emails we send don't include open trackers or click trackers.
  • No selling. We don't sell or rent your information to anyone.

How we use what we collect

  • To run the service: show you your feed, deliver posts, sign you in, send transactional emails.
  • To improve the service: anonymous analytics from PostHog tell us what's working and what isn't.
  • To stay in touch: if you signed up for the waiting list, we'll email you about Craftsky launching. That's it.
  • To meet legal obligations and protect the service from abuse.

Who we share it with

We use a small number of providers to run Craftsky. They process data on our behalf:

  • Brevo — sends our emails (waiting-list announcements, transactional emails). Brevo holds the email addresses we send to. See Brevo's privacy policy.
  • PostHog — anonymous product analytics. PostHog holds the anonymous event data we send. See PostHog's privacy policy.
  • Hosting providers. Cloudflare hosts the website; our application servers run on cloud infrastructure. These providers see the requests they handle.

We don't share your information with anyone else, except where we have to by law (a valid court order, for example).

Your rights

You can:

  • Ask what we hold about you. Email support@craftsky.com.
  • Ask us to delete it. We'll delete the data we hold for you. Note that data on the AT Protocol lives on your PDS, not on Craftsky's servers — we can stop indexing it, but the public records on the network remain yours to delete from your PDS.
  • Take your data with you. That's the point of building on the AT Protocol — your posts and follows are portable. Sign in to any other compatible app and they come with you.
  • Unsubscribe. Every non-essential email has an unsubscribe link.

If you're in the UK or EU, the UK GDPR / EU GDPR gives you specific rights (access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection). All of the above applies; email us to exercise them.

Cookies

The Craftsky website uses no cookies for analytics or advertising. PostHog is configured for memory-only persistence (it forgets you between page loads). The app may store a session token locally so you stay signed in.

We honour the Do Not Track browser signal — if your browser sends DNT, PostHog is not loaded.

Children

Craftsky is not intended for anyone under 13. If you believe a child has given us personal information, email us and we'll delete it.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect what we hold: TLS in transit, encrypted storage at rest, regular dependency updates. No system is perfectly secure; if you spot a vulnerability, please email support@craftsky.com before disclosing it publicly.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy in a meaningful way, we'll let you know — by email if you have an account, or by notice on the site otherwise. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent change.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@craftsky.com.
